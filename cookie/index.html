Cookie notice

Cookies and consent

Forget About uses essential browser storage for account and generator functions, and asks for analytics consent where non-essential measurement is used.

Essential storage

Essential cookies or local storage keep the site working. They may support account sessions, password recovery, saved local UI preferences, launch-preview dismissal, security checks, and checkout return state.

Analytics

Where enabled, analytics help us understand which routes and generator flows are useful. You can choose essential-only mode in the consent banner. Essential functions still work if analytics is declined.

Advertising consent

Google advertising consent is handled by the configured Google consent message where it applies. Forget About's local cookie banner is for analytics consent and does not replace any required advertising consent flow.

Changing your choice

You can clear site data in your browser to reset local consent choices. If you need help, contact support.
