Terms of use

These terms are draft launch copy for the Forget About public preview. They explain how the generator tools, STL downloads, uploaded-print route, print marketplace, and account features are intended to work before formal legal review is complete.

Using the generators

Forget About provides browser-based parametric generators and uploaded-file tools. You are responsible for checking dimensions, materials, compatibility, and whether the generated or uploaded model is suitable for its intended use before printing or ordering.

Generated STL files are supplied for your own permitted use unless a separate written commercial licence is agreed. Do not use the service to upload, generate, request, or distribute unsafe, unlawful, infringing, misleading, abusive, or technically unsuitable models.

Accounts and orders

Accounts let you save designs, uploads, print requests, and order history. Keep your login details secure and tell us promptly if you believe your account has been used without permission.

Checkout is handled through Stripe. Card details are processed by Stripe and are not stored by Forget About. An order is not treated as paid or ready for fulfilment until payment has been confirmed server-side.

Print providers

Print providers are independent businesses. They are responsible for their production quality, listings, capabilities, tax, safety obligations, and customer communication. Forget About may review, suspend, decline, refund, or remove print requests where needed to protect customers, providers, or the platform.

Changes and support

The service is still in launch hardening. Features, routes, prices, provider availability, and policy wording may change before full launch.
