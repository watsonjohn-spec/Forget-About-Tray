Support

How to get help

Use this page to route launch support requests for accounts, generator settings, saved designs, uploaded files, print quotes, provider jobs, payments, refunds, and privacy requests.

Fastest route

Email help@forgetabout.im. Do not send passwords, secret keys, full card details, or private provider credentials.

Email support Refunds and cancellations

Account and privacy help

For login, password, data export, deletion, or privacy requests, include your account email and the route you were using. Order and VAT records may be retained where legally required.

Order and print help

For print orders, include the order reference, provider name if visible, material or colour selected, delivery status, and photos if the issue is physical. Provider payouts remain held until completion rules are satisfied.

Generator help

For STL or preview issues, include the route, generator mode, dimensions, base shape, uploaded-file type if relevant, and whether the issue appears before or after export.
