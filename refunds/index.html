Draft refunds policy

Refunds and cancellations

This draft policy explains how refund and cancellation decisions are expected to work for STL access, uploaded-print orders, and marketplace print jobs during launch hardening.

Before payment

Check the generated preview, dimensions, material assumptions, provider quote, postage, lead time, and delivery details before paying. If something looks wrong before checkout, do not pay yet. Contact support or adjust the design first.

Digital STL access

Digital STL access is normally final once a file is generated, downloaded, or emailed, except where the file is not delivered, is corrupted, materially fails to match the confirmed generator settings, or statutory rights require otherwise.

Print orders

Marketplace print orders can usually be cancelled before the selected provider accepts the job or starts production. Once production starts, refunds or reprints are assessed for non-delivery, damage in transit, incorrect material or colour, significant mismatch against the confirmed design, print failure, or provider error.

Provider payouts remain held until the order reaches completion or the automatic completion rule applies after delivery confirmation chasers.

How to request help

Include your account email, order reference, Stripe payment reference if you have it, photos where relevant, and a short description of the issue.
