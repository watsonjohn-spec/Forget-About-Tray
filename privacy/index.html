Draft privacy notice

Privacy

This draft explains the personal data Forget About expects to handle while running accounts, saved designs, uploaded files, print quotes, orders, provider fulfilment, analytics, and support.

Data we use

  • Account details such as email, first name, second name, profile preferences, and login metadata.
  • Saved designs, uploaded STL metadata, generated parameters, quote requests, orders, refunds, messages, delivery status, and provider records.
  • Support messages, privacy requests, device security signals, analytics consent, and basic site-performance data.

Why we use it

We use this data to run the service, save and reload designs, generate files, match print jobs to providers, process payment and refunds, fulfil orders, prevent abuse, maintain security, improve the platform, and meet legal or accounting duties.

Who handles it

We share only what is needed with infrastructure providers, payment processors, analytics and advertising providers where consent applies, support tools, and the selected print provider. Stripe handles card details. Forget About does not store card numbers.

Your choices

You can ask for access, correction, deletion, portability, or an objection review. Order, VAT, refund, and fulfilment records may need to be retained where the law requires it.
