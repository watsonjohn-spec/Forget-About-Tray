Email help@forgetabout.im

What to include

  • The route you were using, such as Tray, Print, Factory, Makeup, Paint, or Stitch.
  • Your account email, but not your password.
  • Order, Stripe, provider, or quote reference IDs if you have them.
  • For visual or print issues, photos or screenshots and the expected result.

Partnerships and providers

Print providers, suppliers, advertisers, and potential partners can use the same support address. Mark the subject clearly so it can be routed correctly.
